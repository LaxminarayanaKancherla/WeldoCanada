Our Forge

Weld O Canada Inc., a proudly Canadian company, excels in welding, fabrication, and machinery supply from our 22,000 sq. ft facility. Certified for CSA W47.1 (Carbon Steel, Stainless Steel) and CSA W47.2 (Aluminum), we deliver precision and durability.

TSSA Certified: B31.1 (Power Piping), B31.3 (Process Piping), Fabrication, and Installation. We also provide mechanical contractor services and custom machinery solutions.

ISO 9001:2015 certified with an HSE Manual, we ensure safety and quality in every project.

Specializing in ASME pressure vessels, biopharmaceutical equipment, modular skids, vacuum chambers, storage tanks, and custom machinery, we serve industries like biopharmaceutical, semiconductor, alternative energy, and power generation.

Our clients rely on us for precision-engineered components and machinery delivered on time and at competitive prices.
